A slighty more elaborate hello world example where you can say hello to any planet or other subject.
<soft>You can even greet multiple subjects at once.</soft>

Examples:
- <cmd>demo hello mars</cmd>
- <cmd>demo hello jupyter</cmd>
- <cmd>demo hello universe</cmd>
- <cmd>demo hello David</cmd>
- <cmd>demo hello David, Sophia, Billy</cmd>
