Follow-up command for the <cmd>validate molecule(s)</cmd> commands.

This will visualize the molecules in an iframe (Jupyter) or your browser (CLI).

Example:
<cmd>demo validate mols [Acetylcholine,Dopamine,Glutamate,Serotonin,Norepinephrine,GABA,Oxytocin,Estrogen,Testosterone,ARGIPRESSIN,Enkephalin,Dynorphin]</cmd>
<cmd>demo mols visualize</cmd>
